Do you have a strong mathematics background? If you are a nursing major and can complete nearly all of the problems in Exercises 3.2 on your own, you might not be required to take a mathematics course and could be eligible to take a free elective instead. Since you will need to do problems similar to Exercises 3.2 in your nursing courses and licensure exams, this waiver is only granted for students with exceptionally strong math backgrounds. Your academic advisor 1  and the mathematics program director will review your request for a waiver of the mathematics requirement. If you change your major later, you will need to fulfill the mathematics requirement to graduate.
