If you are a nursing major and almost all the above problems seem easy for you, you might not be required to take a mathematics course and could be eligible to take a free elective instead. Since you will need to do similar problems in your nursing courses and licensure exams, this waiver is only granted for students with exceptionally strong math backgrounds. Your academic advisor 1  and the mathematics program director will review your request for a waiver of the mathematics requirement. If you change your major later, you will need to fulfill the mathematics requirement to graduate.
