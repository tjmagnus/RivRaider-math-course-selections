Incoming students with a strong mathematical foundation may take a course above MA130 Functions to fulfill their general education mathematics requirement. Options include MA165 Calculus I, MA166 Calculus II, MA220 Calculus III, MA210 Linear Algebra, and MA310 Discrete Mathematics. Information on these courses may be found in the Rivier University Academic Catalog. Students who believe that they are ready to take a course above should contact their academic advisor 1  who will facilitate a discussion between you and the Mathematics Program director.
