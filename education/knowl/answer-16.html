Answer 7.4.1.

We appreciate your concern and recognize that choosing the right course may seem to be a daunting task. If the course you were initially directed to seems reasonable, we encourage you to sign up for that course. Otherwise, consider the following: As an Education Major, you should first determine whether you need MA100 Math Refresher since that course will help prepare you for your Core Math course, some education courses, and your licensure exams. Taking MA100 Math Refresher is a benefit to the students who really need it, but many students are ready to complete their core math requirement. Education majors who can do most of the skills in MA100 Math Refresher should consider whether they would benefit from MA127 Geometrical Explorations which delves deeper into the geometry needed for K-8 teaching and tested on the licensure exam. MA127 Geometrical Explorations is only offered in the spring semester so Education majors choosing mathematics as their specialization may opt to take MA126 Puzzles, Patterns, and Probability or MA130 Functions in their first semester.
