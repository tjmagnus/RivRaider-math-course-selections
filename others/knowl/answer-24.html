Answer 8.4.1.

We appreciate your concern and recognize that choosing the right course may seem to be a daunting task. If the course you were initially directed to seems reasonable and interesting, we encourage you to sign up for that course. Otherwise, you should first determine whether you need MA100 Math Refresher since that course will help prepare you for your Core Math course, Taking MA100 Math Refresher is a benefit to the students who really need it, but many students are ready to complete their core math requirement. Because your degree plan does not specify a mathematics course, you may choose whichever of the Core Math Requirement courses, MA123 Quantitative Literacy, MA126 Puzzles, Patterns, and Probability, MA127 Geometrical Explorations, or MA130 Functions sounds the most interesting to you.
