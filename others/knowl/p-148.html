Students who have taken an Advanced Placement Test in Calculus or an International Baccalaureate Higher Level Examination may be eligible to receive credit for a mathematics course. Once scores are received, students should contact their academic advisor 1  to get the credit awarded and discuss schedule and degree plan options. Students who are waiting on their scores are encouraged to enroll in MA130 Functions now to reserve a seat and then to adjust their schedule once scores are available.
