Some students need to reinforce their algebraic skills and understanding to prepare for success in MA130. If you are able to do most of the problems in the next set on your own, then you should do well in MA130. If your degree and career plan includes a need for Functions or Calculus and you struggle with most of these prerequisite problems, then you may want to start in MA112. If you do not plan on taking MA130 later, we recommend that you choose one of the other options accessible from the left sidebar.
